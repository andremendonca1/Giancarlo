Gourmet Desserts

Fine and classic desserts with an unparalleled flavor. Enjoy the creamy Gourmet Desserts from the Giancarlo house made to measure by our chef Prasert Jaa, who prudently combines European tradition with Asian sophistication.

Llavine Giancarlo, grandmother of Giancarlo brothers, was the great inventor of almost all of our delicious recipes. With her unforgettable personality and undeniable humor, Llavine created sweet, airy cakes on her family's farm in Nice. Her succulent creations are immortalized in the Giancarlo lineage as well as her.

Petit Gâteau

Llavine Giancarlo's most memorable dessert. Always made with lots of love, yours classic petit gâteau was brought to life again in the hands of our chef, Prasert Jaa, who has managed to perfectly recreate the unforgettable flavor of Llavine's Gâteau.

With small crystallized pieces of pure chocolate inside the gâteau, covered by a thin layer of crunchy dough that melts in contact with the creamy Sicilian lemon ice cream, the Petit Gâteau is finished off with a toffee caramel topping.
