French Gastronomy

Carrying an essence from three generations, Giancarlo bring the best from the french gastronomy into your plate. We are proud for every meal we do, always targeting to maintain our history and legacy untouched.

Giancarlo Vineyards

One of the oldest traditions, nourished generation after generation in the family Giancarlo for more than a century. The famous Giancarlo Vineyards in Nice, south of France, are the most sacrad love of the family, that produces the most wanted wines in the world.

All the prestigious wines from our restaurant's wine house come directly from the vineyards in Nice, where the own Giancarlo's family team make all the process of bottling, keeping the pure fresh smell of their natural wines.
